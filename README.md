## Catalog Microservice

**Description:**

The Catalog Microservice is a core component in a larger software ecosystem designed to manage and provide access to a catalog of products or services. Its primary function is to organize, store, and serve information related to the available products. This microservice offers essential functionalities, including product listing, searching, filtering, and detailed information retrieval. It is responsible for product categorization, pricing, inventory management, and seamless integration with other components such as user carts and order processing. By serving as a centralized source of product data, the Catalog Microservice plays a vital role in ensuring a smooth and efficient shopping experience for users.

**Key Features:**

- **Product Listing:** Displays a comprehensive list of available products, allowing users to browse and explore the catalog.
- **Search and Filtering:** Empowers users to search for specific products and apply filters to refine their search results.
- **Product Details:** Provides in-depth information about each product, including descriptions, images, pricing, and availability.
- **Categorization:** Organizes products into categories or tags for user-friendly navigation.
- **Pricing and Inventory Management:** Manages product prices, stock levels, and real-time availability.
- **Integration:** Seamlessly communicates with other microservices, such as the Cart Microservice, to facilitate a smooth shopping and checkout experience.

The Catalog Microservice serves as a foundational component in e-commerce platforms and various online systems where products or services are offered to users. It ensures that businesses can efficiently manage their product data, making it readily accessible to customers and other parts of the system.
